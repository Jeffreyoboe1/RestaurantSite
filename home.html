Pacileo's is a gluten-free Perfect Health Diet Restaurant based loosely on the Paleo diet. All meals are made to order from fresh, locally sourced, organic ingredients by our master chefs. In place of table sugar, we use natural sweeteners like raw honey or maple syrup. In place of typical wheat flours, we may use a variety of natural flours, including rice, almond, coconut, tapioca, and other flours. We do offer goat milk, almond milk, or coconut milk in place of common dairy milk.
